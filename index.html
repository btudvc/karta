Welcome to B-Less

A lightweight planner for projects, tasks, meetings and visits. We added a few sample items so you can poke around — delete them anytime.

  • Spaces — group your work in the left sidebar (Work, Personal, or your own).
  • Add things — tap the + next to a Space to add a list, meeting, journal or expenses.
  • Tasks — open a list to add tasks, mark them Done, set priorities, attach notes and files.
  • Bottom nav — quick views for Today, Calendar, Visits and Settings across every space.
  • Backup — connect Google Drive from Settings to keep your data safe.
